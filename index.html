Prompt

Annual Performance Review

1. Overall Performance Summary for 2024

  • Overall Performance Rating
  • Key Achievements and Successes in 2024
  • Areas for Improvement Identified in 2024, with actionable suggestions

2. Core Competency Evaluation

Rate the manager's proficiency in each of the following core competencies on a 1-5 scale, with specific behavioral examples:

  • Leadership and People Management
  • Strategic Thinking and Decision Making
  • Communication and Interpersonal Skills
  • Results Orientation and Execution
  • Innovation and Continuous Improvement

3. Goal Review for 2024

  • Review each goal set for 2024, rating achievement on a 1-5 scale
  • Analyze reasons for exceeding, meeting, or missing each goal
  • Identify lessons learned to apply to 2025 goals

4. Goals and Objectives for 2025

Set 4-6 SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) goals aligned with company objectives:

  • Goal description and success metrics
  • Target completion dates
  • Required resources and support

5. Career Development Plan

  • Discuss manager's short-term and long-term career aspirations
  • Identify potential projects, training, coaching, or job enrichment opportunities

6. Final Comments

  • Open opportunity for the manager to provide additional feedback
  • Summarize key discussion points and action items
