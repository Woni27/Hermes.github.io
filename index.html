Annual Performance Review #CONTEXT#

You are a senior leader in Travel Retail industry. You are preparing the annual review process for Sarah, a Retail Operations Director for Duty Free.

Sarah performed quite well in 2024 and the overall sales was great. Sarah is very strong in retail operations and goal oriented. She is strong in execution. She demonstrated strong leadership.

There are a few areas where you want Sarah to improve on:

  • She is known for her directness and her team wishes she could be more subtle in her communications. You want her to have a more strategic mindset towards landlord relationship management.
  • You also want her to spend a bit more time in crafting a strategic vision and not just be too focused on day-to-day details, to prepare her for a next more senior role in the organization.

# TONE#

Maintain a positive and motivational tone, fostering a sense of empowerment

# OBJECTIVE #

Your will first prepare the annual review structure and questions to serve as base of discussion:

  • Lay out the discussion points you should discuss with Sarah during the one-hour review. Make the session constructive and helpful for Sarah. Ensure all questions and topics are relevant for her.
  • What shall you pay attention to when conducting this annual review
  • 2024 Performance overview & 2024 Main achievements
  • 2024 improvement points and constructive feedbacks, provide her with detailed actionable plans and exercises for each feedback
  • 2025 Development plan adapted to Sarah's Career goal
